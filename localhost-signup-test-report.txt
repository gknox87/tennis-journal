3. Event handlers for sport/focus selection may not be properly wired
4. Password field values may not be accessible due to masking

CONCLUSION:
----------
The signup form appears to have a validation issue where button clicks for sport and focus
selection may not be properly updating the React state, causing the form validation to fail
even when all fields appear to be filled. The form validation error message "Please fill in
all fields" suggests that either the sportId or one of the password fields is not being
properly captured by the form state.
