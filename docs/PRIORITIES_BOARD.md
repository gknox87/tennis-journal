## ðŸ’¡ Feature Request Triage

### Intake Process
1. **Collect:** User feedback, support tickets, team ideas
2. **Categorise:** Bug, enhancement, new feature
3. **Prioritise:** Impact Ã— Effort scoring
4. **Decide:** Now, Later, Never
5. **Communicate:** Update requestor

### Prioritisation Criteria
- **User Impact:** How many users benefit?
- **Business Value:** Does it drive retention/revenue?
- **Strategic Fit:** Aligns with roadmap?
- **Effort:** Engineering weeks required
- **Risk:** Technical or business risk level

### Example Scoring
