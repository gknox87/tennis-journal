**Likelihood:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**Likelihood:** Low | **Impact:** Medium  
**Mitigation:**

## ðŸ’¬ Questions & Answers

### Q: Why multi-sport vs. single sport?
**A:** Larger addressable market (10x), less competition, more defensible. We can be #1 in multi-sport vs. #5 in tennis-only.

### Q: How will you compete with free alternatives?
**A:** Superior UX, AI features, social community, and coach tools. Free tier for acquisition, premium for retention.

### Q: What if premium conversion is <8%?
**A:** We've modelled at 5% and still reach profitability by Month 24. Can also increase prices or add B2B revenue.

### Q: Why invest now vs. later?
**A:** Sports tech is growing fast. First-mover advantage in multi-sport. Network effects take time to build.

### Q: What's your unfair advantage?
**A:** AI/ML capabilities, multi-sport data across categories, strong product sense, lean execution.
