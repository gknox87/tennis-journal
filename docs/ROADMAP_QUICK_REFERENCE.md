## ðŸ“… Sprint Planning Guide

### 2-Week Sprint Capacity
- 1 Engineer = ~60 hours productive time
- Account for bugs (10%), meetings (15%), unknowns (15%)
- Effective capacity: ~36 hours per engineer per sprint

### Feature Sizing
- **Small:** 1-3 days (8-24 hours)
- **Medium:** 4-7 days (32-56 hours)
- **Large:** 8-15 days (64-120 hours)
- **Epic:** 16+ days (break down further)

### Sprint Velocity Target
- 1 engineer: 2-3 medium features OR 1 large feature per sprint
- 2 engineers: 4-6 medium features OR 2 large features per sprint
